

code: CNTO 301
name: Basic Cantonese I
cred: 3
desc: Elementary level (part I) course in Cantonese for non-heritage learners with no prior exposure to or background in Cantonese. Focus on training for basic listening and conversational skills in Cantonese. 
preq: CHIN 107 or CHIN 233 or CHIN 108 or CHIN 234. Or placement approval

code: CNTO 303
name: Basic Cantonese II
cred: 3
desc: Elementary level (part II) Cantonese for non-heritage learners. Continuation of CNTO 301. Focus on training for basic listening, conversational, and reading skills. 
preq: CNTO 301. Or placement approval

code: CNTO 311
name: Basic Cantonese for Mandarin Speakers
cred: 3
desc: Basic oral and presentation skills in Cantonese. This is an accelerated course that covers content and topics similar to CNTO 301 and CNTO 303, but is structured specifically for heritage speakers of Mandarin. 
preq: CHIN 243 or CHIN 244. Or placement approval

code: CNTO 401
name: Intermediate Cantonese I
cred: 3
desc: Intermediate lower level (part I) Cantonese for non-heritage learners. 
preq: CNTO 303 or CNTO 311. Or placement approval

code: CNTO 403
name: Intermediate Cantonese II
cred: 3
desc: Intermediate lower level (part II) Cantonese for non-heritage learners. Continuation of CNTO 401. 
preq: CNTO 401. Or placement approval

code: CNTO 451
name: Advanced Cantonese through Popular Culture
cred: 3
desc: Popular culture in Hong Kong from media such as television, cinema, popular music, literature, social media, etc., through advanced authentic Cantonese listening and reading materials.  Open to non-heritage and heritage speakers of Mandarin and/or Cantonese. 
preq: CNTO 401. Prior to registration a minimum of 9 credits of 300-level ASIA coursework is strongly recommended
